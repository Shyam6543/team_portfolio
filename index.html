01 — Identity
Who We Are

GANTS — an acronym forged from our names — is a team of five BTech students at ICFAI University, Hyderabad, united by a shared curiosity for Artificial Intelligence and software development.

We chose the name GANTS because it captures each of us: Geetha, Akshitha, Nishitha, Tejaswi, and Shyam. It's short, sharp, and unforgettable — just like the systems we plan to build.

Fun fact: despite studying AI, none of us initially agreed on which ML framework to use. We spent 20 minutes debating Python libraries before realising we all meant the same thing in different words. Classic GANTS move. 🤓

🎯

Mission

Turn classroom theory into real-world impact through intelligent systems and clean code.

⚡

Vibe

Collaborative, curious, and just competitive enough to push each other further.

🌐

Focus

AI, ML, web dev, and data science — we cover full-stack and full-brain.
